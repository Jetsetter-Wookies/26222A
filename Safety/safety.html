Safety Plan:

  • Don't fly the drone if something looks wrong(Sticking Out Antenna)
  • Update the drone before flying
  • Make sure you try to fly the drone lower before you hold the emergency Stop button(L1) for 3 seconds when you lose control of your drone, otherwise the drone will drop wherever it's at.
  • Check the B4UFLY app(i.e AutoPylot for Drones)
